== Description ==


You may want to show off your site's total number of sold product  on the front-end of your site. This plugin adds a new shortcode
 `[MPCSP]`, that will do just that! it also supports multisite.


**Example Use:**


 You want to brag to your customers how much product you'r store has sold
.
 You want to brag to your customers how much product you'r each store has sold 
in case of multisite. 

The Shortcode: `[MPCSP]`



== Installation ==

1. Either download the plugin and upload it or search for it under Plugins > Add New. Activate it, too.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Use the [MPCSP] shortcode in post or pages 
4. Place `<?php echo do_shortcode('[MPCSP]'); ?>` in your templates
5. Be awesome!
